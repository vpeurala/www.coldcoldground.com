h3(#n_10_1_2013). Request for video clips

Now you all have a chance to be a part of Cold Cold Ground's next music video. 

What we need:
Short video clips of friends and fans listening to "Disintegrating" with their earphones on somewhere in their own environment: on the street, on the bus, at home, in the woods, on the shore, whatsoever... The place doesn't matter so much, instead what matters is that the video shows YOU with earphones. You can be incognito as well if you want. These video files can be shot by any camera, mobile phone etc. and can be in any video format (QuickTime (.mov) preferred, though). Please keep the videos short, the perfect length would be 3-10 seconds.

Email the files to: nooz@coldcoldground.com before January 15th 2013. Please also mention the name you want to be shown in the thanks list and include a postal address where your fabulous surprise package can be sent to.

Thank you so much for your help!

h3(#n_25_4_2012). CCG supporting Turmion k&auml;til&ouml;t at Tavastia on 18.5.

Cold Cold Ground will play at Tavastia, Helsinki, on Friday 18.5.2012, supporting their labelmates from "Osasto-A Records":http://www.osastoa.com, "Turmion k&auml;til&ouml;t":http://www.turmionkatilot.com. More official information about the event is available on "Tavastia's website":http://www.tavastiaklubi.fi/tapahtuma/23258. Buy your tickets in advance from "Tiketti":http://www.tiketti.fi/Turmion-Katilot-Tavastia-klubi-Helsinki-lippuja/15411.

Also, CCG's percussionist Kunt has left the band due to time constraints and him wanting to concentrate on his other band "V For Violence":http://www.vforviolence.com/. There is no drama behind Kunt's departure and everybody will remain friends.
