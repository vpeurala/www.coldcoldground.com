Kunt has already performed with CCG a couple of times, but the first official live show of the new lineup will be at K17, Berlin, Germany on 7.9.2011.

h3(#n_13_6_2011). CCG plays at Findustry 2011

Findustry 2011 is a new festival focused on Finnish industrial/alternative metal. The two-day event will be held at Green Room (Helsinki) from Friday 12.8. to Saturday 13.8., with four bands performing each night. Cold Cold Ground is the headliner of Saturday night, expected showtime for CCG is half past midnight. The first band starts at 22:00.

Be there! The seven other bands are really good too. This is going to be Helsinki's number one industrial event in 2011.
